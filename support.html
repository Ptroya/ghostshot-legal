Troubleshooting Guide

Camera Access Issues

If the camera is not functioning within the app:

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Navigate to Privacy & Security → Camera
  3. Ensure GhostShot has permission enabled
  4. Force close and restart the application
  5. If the issue persists, restart your device

Photo Saving Problems

If photos are not being saved properly:

  • Verify that a category has been selected before capturing
  • Check available storage space on your device (Settings → General → iPhone Storage)
  • Confirm you are in an active capture session
  • Force close and reopen the application
  • Check that you have not exceeded category limits

Automatic Deletion Issues

If photos are being deleted unexpectedly or not deleting as scheduled:

  • Review retention settings for the specific category (tap category → Settings)
  • Remember that photos are automatically deleted based on your configured retention period
  • Check the Recycle Bin for recently deleted photos (24-hour recovery window)
  • To preserve important photos permanently, use the save function to export them to your Photos library
  • Verify the app is not being force-closed by the system, which may interrupt deletion schedules

Photo Library Access

If you cannot save photos to your device's Photos library:

  1. Open device Settings
  2. Go to Privacy & Security → Photos
  3. Select GhostShot from the app list
  4. Set permission to "Add Photos Only" or "Full Access"
  5. Return to GhostShot and attempt to save again

Application Crashes or Performance Issues

If the app crashes, freezes, or exhibits poor performance:

  1. Force close the application (swipe up from app switcher)
  2. Reopen GhostShot
  3. If problem continues, restart your device
  4. Check for available iOS updates (Settings → General → Software Update)
  5. Ensure you have the latest version of GhostShot from the App Store
  6. If issue persists after these steps, contact support with crash details

OCR Text Recognition Not Working

If the text recognition feature is not functioning correctly:

  • Ensure the photo contains clear, legible text
  • Check that lighting conditions in the photo are adequate
  • Verify your device supports on-device text recognition (iOS 14.0 or later)
  • Try capturing the text at a different angle or distance
  • The feature works best with printed text rather than handwriting

Frequently Asked Questions

How long are photos retained in the app?

Photos are automatically deleted based on your configured retention settings. Available retention periods include:

  • 30 minutes
  • 3 hours
  • 1 day (default setting)
  • 3 days
  • 1 week
  • Delete immediately after sharing

You can configure a global default retention period or customize the setting for each individual category.

Is it possible to recover deleted photos?

Photos deleted from categories are moved to the Recycle Bin, where they remain accessible for 24 hours. After this period expires, photos are permanently deleted and cannot be recovered through any means.

To preserve a photo beyond its scheduled deletion time, use the save function to export it to your device's Photos library before it is deleted.

Does GhostShot back up my photos to cloud storage?

No. All photos are stored exclusively on your local device within the application's private storage. No cloud backup, synchronization, or server upload occurs. This design ensures maximum privacy and security.

If you wish to back up important photos, manually save them to your Photos library, which can then be backed up through iCloud or other backup services you use.

Why doesn't GhostShot automatically save photos to the Camera Roll?

GhostShot is designed specifically for temporary photo storage. Photos remain private within the application and do not automatically clutter your Camera Roll or Photos library. This design provides better organization and privacy control.

You can manually save any photo to your Camera Roll at any time by using the save button in the photo viewer.

Can I synchronize photos across multiple devices?

No. GhostShot does not support multi-device synchronization or cloud storage. Photos remain exclusively on the device where they were captured. This design choice prioritizes privacy and security by eliminating data transmission and storage on external servers.

How many categories can I create?

The application allows creation of up to 5 custom categories (this is the default limit and may be configurable in settings). Each category supports independent configuration of:

  • Category name
  • Icon selection
  • Color scheme
  • Retention period

How does GhostShot protect my privacy?

GhostShot implements several privacy-focused design principles:

  • All photos stored locally on device in private application storage
  • No data transmission to external servers (except anonymized crash reports)
  • No collection of personal information
  • No advertising or tracking
  • Automatic deletion based on user-defined schedules
  • Optional export to Photos library under user control

For complete details, please review our Privacy Policy.

What is Quick Capture Mode?

Quick Capture Mode is a convenience feature that bypasses the category selection screen and opens the camera directly. When enabled:

  • The app uses your most recently selected category
  • Previously configured retention settings are applied
  • Camera opens immediately upon app launch

This mode can be enabled or disabled from the category selection screen.

How does the OCR text recognition feature work?

GhostShot includes on-device Optical Character Recognition (OCR) powered by Apple's Vision framework. This feature:

  • Extracts text from photos entirely on your device
  • Does not transmit images or text to external servers
  • Works best with clear, well-lit printed text
  • Stores recognized text alongside the photo in local storage
  • Allows copying extracted text to clipboard

Text recognition is subject to the same automatic deletion schedule as the associated photo.

How do I change the application language?

To change the interface language:

  1. Open the application Settings menu
  2. Locate the Language option
  3. Select your preferred language (English or Spanish)
  4. The interface will update immediately

What happens to my data if I uninstall the app?

When you uninstall GhostShot from your device, all photos, categories, settings, and associated data are permanently deleted. This data cannot be recovered after uninstallation.

If you plan to reinstall the app and wish to preserve certain photos, save them to your Photos library before uninstalling.
