Data Storage and Processing

Local Storage Only

All photos and data created within GhostShot are stored exclusively on your device in the app's private sandbox. This means:

  • Your photos never leave your device unless you explicitly share them
  • No cloud storage or backup services are used by the app
  • All data is isolated from other apps on your device
  • When you delete the app, all associated data is permanently removed

Photo Access

GhostShot requires camera access to capture photos. The app:

  • Only accesses the camera when you actively use the capture feature
  • Stores captured photos in the app's private storage (not in your Photos library)
  • Only accesses your Photos library when you choose to save a photo (optional feature)
  • Cannot access existing photos in your library without your explicit permission

Automatic Deletion

Photos in GhostShot are automatically deleted based on your chosen retention period (30 minutes to 1 week, or delete after sharing). This feature:

  • Runs locally on your device
  • Does not require internet connection
  • Cannot be recovered once deleted (except within the 24-hour recycle bin period)

User Settings and Preferences

The app stores your preferences (language, theme, retention defaults, etc.) using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults). This information:

  • Remains on your device
  • Is not transmitted or backed up to our servers
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

GhostShot does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app uses only native Apple frameworks.

Data Security

While we do not collect data, we take security seriously:

  • All photos are stored in the app's sandboxed directory, inaccessible to other apps
  • No data transmission occurs (no servers to breach)
  • Photos are automatically purged based on your retention settings
  • Optional recycle bin provides 24-hour recovery window for accidental deletions

Children's Privacy

GhostShot does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages as it does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Permissions Required

GhostShot may request the following permissions:

  1. Camera Access (Required): To capture photos within the app
  2. Photo Library Access (Optional): Only when you choose to save photos to your device

You can revoke these permissions at any time through iOS Settings.
